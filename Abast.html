Abstract

In this review, we give an answer of the important questions: What are operating systems, how can they operate and what benefit we catch up from them and to what extent? We give examples of the most common operating systems, like Microsoft windows, MacOs, UNIX, Linux, Apple’s iOS and Google’s Android. We reviewed, in some detail, the common jobs of these operating systems related to device hardware and applications. We showed that, without operating systems, all current and future devices that depend on modern technology will be useless.
